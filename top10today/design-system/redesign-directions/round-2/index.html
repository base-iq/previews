Direction D

The Ledger

No accent color at all. Ink on paper, one serious serif for the numerals, franklin gothic for everything else — the countdown as a broadsheet ledger where scale and weight do all the talking. The mark is the numero: № 10.

What every direction keeps

  • The countdown. Every ranked list descends; rank 1 renders last, at every size.
  • The finale staging. One marked #1 per surface — intro stamp, the system's single emphatic device, the one display-face sentence.
  • Paid placements are ranked items. Asterisk register, disclosure in the fine print.
  • The broadcast voice. Intro stamps, sentence case, headlines that never exclaim. Copy untouched.
  • Accessibility floor. AA+ on all working text, 44px touch targets, motion stays rationed.
