A few common questions

I'm promoting an event, not a business.

Events have their own flow — dates, venues, ticket links. Same per-day pricing, same editor review. Head to event placements →

Who writes the copy?

You tell us about your business in the form; our local editor shapes it into the directory line and the newsletter paragraph, in the voice readers know. You'll see the final copy in your approval email before the first run.

What happens when a day sells out?

Two pinned spots per city, per day — that's firm. Sold-out days show crossed-off on the calendar when you book, so you'll never pay for a day that's full.

How does pricing across locations work?

Per location, per day. Five days in Glendale is $50 for directory only, or $125 for directory + newsletter. Add a second city and it doubles. The price stays linear — no tiers, no surprises.

Who reviews placements?

A BaseLocal editor in your city. We approve most businesses; we turn down anything that doesn't fit the audience. If yours is declined, you get a full refund.

Can I cancel?

Cancel up to 48 hours before any day's run for a full refund of that day. Inside the 48-hour window, the day is locked in.

How long does review take?

Most placements are approved within 24 hours. We'll email you when yours is live.
