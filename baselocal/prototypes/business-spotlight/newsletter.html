Sand-brewed, the old way: Little Ararat Coffee pulls Armenian coffee from hot sand the way it's been done for centuries, and the walnut gata is gone most mornings by ten. Order at the counter, take the window seat, and watch Brand Boulevard wake up. Worth setting an alarm for.

The staff-picks wall hasn't missed: Brand Books stocks used and new side by side, and the hand-written shelf notes read like recommendations from a friend who knows your taste. Their author events list for June just went up.
Weekend birria, worth planning around: La Cocina de Memo only runs the birria Saturday and Sunday, and regulars start lining up before the doors open. Go early, get the consommé, thank us later.
Sunday yoga, pay what you can: Verdugo Yoga Collective holds donation-based community classes every Sunday morning — mats provided, beginners welcome, zero pressure.
