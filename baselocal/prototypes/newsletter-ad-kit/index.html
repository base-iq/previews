Your ad runs inside an email people actually read.

Every edition is a curated local newsletter: events, news, local spots, written city by city, sent every morning. Most of it is editorial, and the recommendations around your placement are run by our house team.

One placement is sold direct each morning, with a choice of position: the Lead Feature, Inline Feature 1, or both as a combo. All of it sits in the front half of the email.
