A BaseLocal editor in your location will review your event within 24 hours. You'll get an email when it's approved and live — and another receipt copy is on its way to your inbox.

What happens next

  • Your event sits in the editor's review queue. Most submissions are approved within a day.
  • If anything's unclear, the editor will email you directly using the contact info you provided.
  • On approval, you'll get a confirmation email with your live links and a calendar of which days the placement runs.
  • You can edit time, copy, and image up until the run starts. Date and location changes go through the editor.
