A few common questions

Who reviews submissions?

A BaseLocal editor in your location. We approve most submissions; we turn down anything that doesn't fit the audience. If your event is rejected, you get a full refund.

How does pricing across locations work?

Per event, per location, per day. A three-day market in Tacoma and Olympia is six units — $60 for directory only, or $150 for directory + newsletter. The price stays linear.

Can I edit the event after I submit?

Yes — minor edits to time, copy, or image any time before the run starts. Date and location changes go through the editor.

Can I cancel?

Cancel up to 48 hours before a day's run for a full refund of that day. Inside the 48-hour window, the day is locked in.

How long does review take?

Most submissions are approved within 24 hours. We'll email you when your event is live.
