News Updates — above-the-fold explorations

Working doc for differentiating the second daily send from the morning roundup. The current shared header first, then the proposed "News Updates" header (typographic nameplate, no images, no weather) and a bolder pairing with the headline ledger. The three original directions follow for reference. Constraint driving v2: the cover banner is a per-location image (500+ locations), so the news header must be pure text and CSS. Everything uses existing newsletter tokens; the new components (nameplate, headline ledger, card accent rule) are proposals and would go through extend-design-system before production.

Cover photo is the Glendale placeholder from the 7xv1 template — swap per location as usual.

Direction C (reference)

Headlines first

Restructures the IA instead of the chrome: photo shrinks to a strip, the greeting goes, and a numbered headline ledger fills the fold. Dense and scannable where the morning is warm and conversational — the pacing itself is the differentiator. Sponsor copy moves below the ledger.
