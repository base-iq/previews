This weekend in Kitsap County

Our editors' picks — the ones worth planning around. All 20 weekend events →

Silverdale Whaling Days

Sat–Sun, Jul 25–26 · Silverdale Waterfront Park · Free

Fifty-plus years running and still the county's best people-watching. The grand parade rolls at 10, the street fair sprawls along the waterfront all day, and fireworks close Saturday night over Dyes Inlet. Locals park at the mall and walk down.

An Evening with Lee Ann Womack

Sat, Jul 25 · 7:30pm · Admiral Theatre, Bremerton · from $35

The Admiral's art-deco room suits her voice — close, warm, no bad seats. Doors at 6 if you want the dinner service downstairs.

Concerts on the Bay: The Olson Bros Band

Sun, Jul 26 · 5pm · Waterfront Park, Silverdale · Free

Lawn chairs, food trucks, and country harmonies while the sun drops behind the Olympics. The breeze turns cool by the second set — bring a layer.
