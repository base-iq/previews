04 · Spacing · Radius · Rules & Shadows

Depth comes from rules, not shadows

4px base scale. Radius ceiling is 6px — cards and callouts are square; only buttons, inputs (4px) and standalone images (6px) round at all. Shadows are banned except one overlay token for elements genuinely floating above the page (sticky bars, dropdowns) — never on cards, never on hover.

Front-matter divider — 64px centered hairline, once per article, between hero and body

05 · Motion

Ink dries. It doesn't leap.

Entrances are fade-only — opacity, no translate, no scale, no lifts. ease-settle cubic-bezier(.25,1,.5,1) at 0.5s, with a modest 60ms stagger allowed on lists. Micro-transitions (0.15s) apply to color and underline only — nothing moves on hover. The one permitted transform: buttons press down 1px when active, like type hitting paper. All of it collapses under prefers-reduced-motion.

16 · Photography

Wire-photo sensibility, not stock gloss

Approved. Documentary imagery, natural light, muted warm tones. People 50+ as capable subjects. Every editorial photo takes a cutline (§ 08). No illustration systems, no 3D renders, no emoji in editorial surfaces.
